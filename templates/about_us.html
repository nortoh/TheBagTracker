About Us

The Bag Tracker is an online cryptocurrency portfolio tracker / trading interface created by Christopher Hinchey, Christian "Nortoh" Horton, and Richard Molina in 36 caffeine-fueled hours for ShellHacks 2021's CoinRoutes, Inc. challenge.

Developed in Python, HTML, CSS, & JavaScript using Django, Bootstrap, SQLite, and the CoinRoutes Smart Order Router API.
